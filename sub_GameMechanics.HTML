Brimming with Style, Blood is Fuel, Return to sender

Style Points

The more variety of weapons you use, the more points you gain

The more you combo with you weapons, the more stylish you can be.
Doing unique interactions such as parrying, interuptions or using map hazards will boost your points.

Health Mechanics

Blood is your source of Fuel, Soak yourself in blood to gain health to keep going

When talking damage, a portion of your health turns grey, you cant heal unless you move up a style ranking.
Fight up close and personal to not only make good use of your shotgun, you can heal more.

Enemy Interaction

Parry back your opponents attacks by punching them or giving some spare change

Red projectiles or projectiles in general can be parried back for a skillful display of timing.
Hitscan based attacks are the most trickiest attack to parry back since you can only send it back with some spare change.
